1. Acceptance of terms

By using HK Global Market & Services, you agree to use the platform responsibly and legally.

  • You must provide truthful information when publishing an advertisement or contacting another user.
  • You must not use the website for fraud, misleading offers, harassment, spam, or illegal activity.
  • If you do not agree with these terms, you should stop using the platform.

2. Platform role

HK Global Market & Services is a listing and communication platform.

Important clarification

The website helps users publish, search, save, and contact each other. The platform is not automatically the seller, buyer, broker, transport company, repair provider, school, or payment guarantor.

  • Deals are made directly between users unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.
  • Users must verify identity, ownership, documents, prices, availability, quality, and legal status before any deal.
  • The platform may remove suspicious, incomplete, misleading, unsafe, or illegal ads.

3. Publishing advertisements

Every advertisement must match its real category and must include clear and correct details.

  • Vehicle ads must include correct brand, model, year, mileage, fuel or power type, condition, price, and ownership information.
  • Real estate ads must clarify whether the property is for sale, rent, room, land, farm, or commercial use.
  • Service ads must describe the service area, price method, experience, availability, and whether the service is for individuals, homes, or companies.
  • Education and digital service ads must avoid false certificates, fake qualifications, copied portfolios, or unrealistic promises.
  • Photos must belong to the actual item or service whenever possible and must not mislead buyers.
Good ads are detailed, honest, organized, and written in the language suitable for the selected country.

4. Category-specific conditions

Different categories need different information and different safety checks.

Vehicles

Private cars, rental cars, motorcycles, bicycles, small trucks, heavy trucks, and parts must be described accurately.

Real estate

Homes, rooms, shops, land, farms, and commercial spaces require clear ownership, location, size, and contract details.

Electronics

Phones, computers, appliances, gaming, TV, and audio items must show condition, warranty, defects, and included accessories.

Services

Maintenance, repair, cleaning, transport, education, digital work, and personal services must be clear about price and responsibility.

5. Prohibited content

Some content is not allowed because it can be unsafe, illegal, misleading, or harmful.

Do not publish illegal products, stolen goods, fake documents, weapons, drugs, dangerous materials, hateful content, adult exploitation, scams, fake jobs, or any listing that violates the law of the selected country.
  • Do not publish items you do not own or cannot legally sell.
  • Do not publish services that require a license unless you legally hold the required permission.
  • Do not publish ads that copy another person’s photos, company name, brand identity, or intellectual property.

6. Payments, meetings, and transactions

Users should protect themselves before sending money or transferring ownership.

  • Avoid paying before seeing the item, verifying the seller, or checking the required documents.
  • For vehicles and real estate, verify ownership papers, registration, tax duties, debts, damages, and legal restrictions.
  • For services, agree clearly on scope, price, deadline, cancellation, warranty, and responsibility before work begins.
  • Meet in safe public places when possible and avoid suspicious payment methods.

7. Privacy and local browser data

Some information may be stored locally in your browser to make the website easier to use.

  • Country choice, language choice, saved ads, favorites, messages, and account form data may be stored in local browser storage.
  • If you clear browser data, change browser, or use another device, local saved data may disappear.
  • Do not publish private information that you do not want other users to see.

Personal data caution

Users should share only necessary contact information and should avoid publishing sensitive personal, financial, or identity data inside public advertisements.

8. Reports, removal, and changes

The platform may update rules and remove unsafe or unsuitable content.

  • Users may report suspicious ads, fraud attempts, wrong categories, harmful messages, or illegal content.
  • The platform may edit, hide, reject, or remove ads that break rules or harm trust and safety.
  • Terms may be updated as the website grows, adds countries, adds categories, or improves safety systems.
These terms are designed to support a safer global marketplace for vehicles, real estate, electronics, home items, businesses, education, digital services, maintenance, and personal services.
